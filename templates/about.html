About us

We are here to help you and your pets!
If you ever feel busy and can't take care of your pet then you chose the right place.
If you have lots of free time and wish to have a pet to take care of then you chose the right place.
We help petowners to find other petowners or normal people nearby to care for their pets when they can't.
The petsitter will be given also money for that but it won't be much since he/she can always agree on a price with the petowner.
